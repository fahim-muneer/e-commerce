Our Genesis

From a Small Workshop to Your Home.

Banus Home Furnishings began in 2025 in a small, sunlit workshop with a simple idea: furniture should be beautiful, durable, and kind to the planet. Frustrated by mass-produced, disposable pieces, our founder, Elias Vance, sought to bring the principles of **timeless craftsmanship** back to the modern consumer.

We started with a single, perfectly designed dining table. Today, our collection has grown, but our commitment remains the same: every piece is ethically sourced, lovingly crafted, and built to be passed down, not thrown away. We believe in quality that transcends trends.

Core Philosophy

The Values That Shape Every Piece.

Sustainable Sourcing

We use only certified woods and recycled materials, ensuring minimal environmental impact from forest to finish.

Timeless Craft

Our pieces are designed for longevity, using classic joinery techniques and rigorous quality checks that reject fleeting trends.

Full Transparency

We share the origin of our materials and the process of construction so you know exactly where your furniture comes from.
